How to make these small devices of drenching destruction

Luckily no permit is required. Follow the steps below:

  1. Acquire water balloons. The more the better.
  2. Aqcuire water source, preferably an outside.
  3. Attach water balloon to nozzle.
  4. Fill balloon. Make sure to leave a small amount of air inside. This helps assure proper detonation.
  5. Tie the end of the balloon.
  6. Repeat as many times as you possibly can. This is an arms race.
  7. Revel in your new arsenal and picture your rivals bowing to you in cold puddles of defeat!
